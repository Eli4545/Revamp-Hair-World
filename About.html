About Us

As we aim to go professional we see our three year experience in the market as a spring board to make Slimky Hair a one-stop shop to add to every individual beauty needs. Slimky Revamp hair Care Product is the brain child of Mrs Deborah Emmanuel,an entepreneur with passion for beauty and hair care.
